This Month's Cause

In honor of Autism Awareness Month, we are contributing 25% of all sales to the Organization for Autism Research, whose mission is to apply research to the challenges of autism.

The Organization for Autism Research recognizes that individuals with autism, their families, teachers, caregivers, and service providers need better answers to many and varied, pressing questions. They do this through raising money and funding research targeting the immense challenges facing autism.
