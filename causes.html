We Care

Jackson Lane exists as a way for us to raise funds for giving back to social causes, specifically related to healthcare related charities. For as long as we exist, at least 10% of all sales go to a cause we (you and us) care about. We promise to be transparent with our sales and where all the funds are going.

Shop to Support our Cause!

Mental Health Awareness

May 1, 2021 / Causes

In honor of Mental Health Awareness Month, we are contributing 25% of all sales to Brave Trails and the upcoming launch of their mental health program for LGBTQ+ youth.

Autism Awareness Month

April 1, 2021 / Causes

In honor of Autism Awareness Month, we are contributing 25% of all sales to the Organization for Autism Research, whose mission is to apply research to the challenges of autism.

Heart Awareness Month

February 1, 2021 / Causes

In honor of Heart Awareness Month, we are contributing 25% of all sales to Gift of Life International, who provides hope to children with heart disease in 80 emerging countries.

100% of Sales to Liam

November 12, 2020 / Causes

Through the end of 2020, 100% of sales will go to Liam. We are donating all of the materials and labor for all soap purchased.

We Are Back - With a Mission!

November 1, 2020 / Mission

Growing out of a joy of making soap, what drives us is our social mission and giving to others.
