Stay Strong Liam

January, 2021

Liam Sebastian Marquez was born a healthy baby boy on April 16, 2020. On June 19th, precious, little Liam had been diagnosed with a cancerous tumor - Rhabdomyoscarcoma. To date, Liam has gone through five months of chemo treatments and has just completed six weeks of radiation. Through all this, he continues to be all smiles and laughs. He gives such joy to everyone around him. Liam has been a fighter, a superhero, and a warrior through this journey and will continue the fight.

We donated 100% of sales in December, 2020 and 25% of sales during January, 2021. This resulted in $2,443 raised to support Liam's journey.


Learn more about liam at his website.
